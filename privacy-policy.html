Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Claude Code Primer ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website at claude-code-primer.franzai.com (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our Service:

  • Log Information: Server logs including IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our Service, including pages accessed and time spent
  • Device Information: Information about your device, including browser type and version

2.2 Information You Provide

We do not currently collect any personal information that you voluntarily provide, as our Service is a static educational resource.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining our Service
  • Monitoring usage patterns and improving our Service
  • Ensuring security and preventing abuse
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With service providers who assist in operating our Service (subject to confidentiality agreements)

5. Data Retention

We retain log information for up to 30 days for security and operational purposes. After this period, logs are automatically deleted.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your personal information

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our Service does not use cookies or tracking technologies. We do not embed third-party analytics, advertising, or social media widgets that could track your activity.

8. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Security headers to prevent common attacks
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited data collection and retention

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international transfers.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

12. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: privacy@franzai.com

Website: claude-code-primer.franzai.com

13. Legal Basis for Processing (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, our legal basis for collecting and processing your personal information depends on the specific context:

  • Legitimate Interests: For security monitoring and service improvement
  • Legal Compliance: When required by applicable laws
  • Consent: When you provide explicit consent

14. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to request deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights
